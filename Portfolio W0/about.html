About Me:

I am Apurva Kashish, a college student currently studying Metallurgical Engineering in IIT BHU. While engineering is not really my passion, writing is what drives me. Writing poems and stories works as a therapy for me and I feel freer to express what there's in my mind which cannot be spoken out. When I was in class 11th, I started writing and publishing my content on a popular online publishing app, Pratilipi. I received a lot appreciation from the platform and took part in writing competitions. My novel "I Wish I Were A Boy!" came 7th in the contest "Women of Wonders" hosted by Pratilipi.
